Welcome to Samra

Founded over a decade ago by Samra Mersiehazen, Authentic Samra Leather is driven by a vision to offer high-quality leather accessories with urban flair, empowering both the wearer and the Ethiopian community. Handcrafted by skilled artisans, our products are meticulously made from the finest materials, embodying the elegance of simplicity and the strength of premium leather.

Our range, from timeless classics to contemporary interpretations, caters to diverse tastes and occasions.

Committed to originality and sustainable practices, we operate within an eco-friendly framework, ensuring minimal waste by repurposing every scrap into small accessories and floor mats. This dedication not only reduces our environmental impact but also infuses each piece with distinctive character, reflecting our commitment to innovation and eco-consciousness.

Experience the perfect fusion of luxury and functionality with Samra Leather. Explore our collection today and discover the difference firsthand.
